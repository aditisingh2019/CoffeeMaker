Welcome to Brew Haven Coffee

Our Story




As a group of hard-working, Computer Science students, we often found ourselves craving authentic coffee on campus. However, our options were typically limited. As a result, we built Brew Haven, an online interface that brings the ambiance of a coffee shop to the comfort of your computer. Customers are able to log into their accounts using our seamless website to order delicious drinks and view the status of their orders. Staff members are able to log in and create recipes for customers, providing them with delicious drinks based on the available inventory. Our interface helps connect college students to affordable coffee options.
